Old tax regime slabs

  • Up to Rs. 2.5 lakh: nil for normal individuals.
  • Rs. 2.5-5 lakh: 5%.
  • Rs. 5-10 lakh: 20%.
  • Above Rs. 10 lakh: 30%.
  • Senior citizen and super senior citizen basic exemption differs.
Calculator guide

Surcharge and cess

  • Rs. 50 lakh to Rs. 1 crore: surcharge review needed.
  • Rs. 1 crore to Rs. 2 crore: higher surcharge band.
  • Rs. 2 crore plus: regime-specific caps and marginal relief must be reviewed.
  • Health and education cess is generally 4% on tax plus surcharge.

When to use a CA to optimise

  • Income between Rs. 10-20 lakh where old versus new comparison is close.
  • Large capital gains pushing income into surcharge bands.
  • Business income with actual expenses.
  • ESOP, foreign income or multi-employer salary cases.
