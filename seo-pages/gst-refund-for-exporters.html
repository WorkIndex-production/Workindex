Questions People Ask

FAQs

How long does IGST refund take?

Timelines vary; clean GSTR-1 and shipping-bill matching improves speed.

What is a deficiency memo?

It is a GST officer memo asking for correction or additional documents before refund proceeds.

Can I claim ITC refund with domestic sales too?

Yes in some cases, but eligible turnover and formula must be computed.

Can WorkIndex help old stuck refunds?

Yes. Post GSTIN, period, refund ARN and mismatch reason.
