Form 15G and 15H
Avoid unnecessary TDS when eligible

Form 15G and 15H help eligible taxpayers request no TDS deduction where total tax liability is nil or within applicable conditions.

15GIndividuals below 60 and HUFsDeclaration for no TDS where eligibility conditions are met.

Blog

When to submit

  • At the start of the financial year to banks.
  • Before PF withdrawal if applicable.
  • To relevant payer for interest or dividend where allowed.
  • Fresh declaration is usually needed each year.
Blog

What happens if you do not submit

The bank or payer may deduct TDS. You can claim it in ITR if eligible, but refund may take time and requires correct AIS/Form 26AS reporting.

FAQs

Can everyone submit Form 15G?

No. Eligibility conditions must be satisfied.

Does Form 15H apply to senior citizens?

Yes, Form 15H is for eligible senior citizens.

Can TDS already deducted be reversed?

Usually you claim credit in ITR; payer reversal depends on timing and system.
