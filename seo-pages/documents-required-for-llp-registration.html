Documents checklist

LLP registration needs partner-level and entity-level documents

Each designated partner needs identity, address and digital signing readiness. The LLP also needs office proof, name options, business activity and agreement terms.

Documents checklist

Partner documents for each designated partner

  • PAN card for Indian partners.
  • Aadhaar card for Indian residents.
  • Passport for NRI or foreign national partners.
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Personal bank statement or utility bill as address proof.
  • DPIN/DIN details where already available.
  • Digital Signature Certificate.
Documents checklist

Registered office documents

  • Rent agreement in the name of LLP or authorised person.
  • NOC from property owner.
  • Utility bill not older than the usual acceptable period.
  • Sale deed or property-tax receipt for owned premises.
Documents checklist

Entity-level documents

  • At least two proposed LLP name options.
  • Nature of business and main objects.
  • Partner contribution amount and profit-sharing ratio.
  • LLP agreement draft.
Documents checklist

What happens after documents are ready

  • Apply for DPIN/DIN where needed.
  • Reserve name through RUN-LLP.
  • File FiLLiP.
  • File LLP agreement in Form 3 within prescribed time.
  • Apply for PAN/TAN and open bank account.

Can I use home address as LLP registered office?

Often yes if proper NOC, utility bill and ownership/rent proof are available.

What if a partner does not have DPIN?

The professional can guide whether it is applied during incorporation or separately.

Can an NRI be designated partner?

Yes, subject to documents, apostille/notarisation and resident partner requirements.
