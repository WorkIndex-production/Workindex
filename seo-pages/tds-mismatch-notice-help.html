Problem help

What causes TDS mismatch notices

  • TRACES demand for short deduction or short payment.
  • Challan not mapped to correct PAN, quarter or section.
  • Deductee PAN invalid or updated wrongly.
  • Rate applied lower than prescribed.
  • TDS deposited but not included in return.
Problem help

Situations this page is built for

  • TRACES short-payment demand.
  • Form 26AS mismatch reported by employee or vendor.
  • Wrong PAN or challan in filed return.
  • TDS deducted not visible in deductee Form 26AS.
  • Correction return rejected.

Documents usually required

  • Original TDS return acknowledgement.
  • TRACES demand notice or justification report reference.
  • Challan details and BSR code.
  • Deductee PAN list.
  • Form 26AS of affected deductees.

Questions People Ask

FAQs

Can a TDS correction return be filed after years?

Correction may be possible, but availability and records should be checked on TRACES.

What is a TRACES justification report?

It is a report showing demand or mismatch details for a TDS statement.

Is TDS demand the same as income tax demand?

No. TDS demand is usually against the deductor/TAN, while income tax demand is against a taxpayer/PAN.

How quickly can a consultant help?

Simple challan or PAN corrections can be reviewed quickly if documents and portal access are ready.
