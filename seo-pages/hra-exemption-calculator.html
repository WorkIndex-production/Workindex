Find your HRA tax exemption in seconds

HRA exemption under Section 10(13A) is the minimum of actual HRA, rent paid minus 10% of salary, and 50%/40% of salary depending on city.

Formula used

Exempt HRA = Minimum of: actual HRA received; rent paid minus 10% of basic salary; 50% of basic in Delhi/Mumbai/Chennai/Kolkata or 40% elsewhere.
Calculator

Metro cities for HRA

Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai and Kolkata are treated as metro cities for the 50% salary condition. Other cities generally use 40%.

Calculator

What you need to claim HRA

  • Rent receipts.
  • Landlord PAN if annual rent exceeds Rs. 1 lakh.
  • Rent agreement.
  • Form 12BB or employer proof submission.
Calculator

Common HRA mistakes

  • Claiming HRA while living in own house.
  • Missing landlord PAN where required.
  • Wrong metro classification.
  • Not claiming in ITR when employer missed it.
