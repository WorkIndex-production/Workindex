How to Check ITR Refund Status
From ITR processing to refund credit - what to track

Refund status depends on e-verification, processing, bank validation, demand adjustment and refund reissue steps.

How-to

Refund follows a specific sequence

  • Return filed and e-verified.
  • Return processed and refund determined.
  • Refund issued to bank.
  • Bank credits amount in 2-5 working days after issue in many cases.
  • If not credited, bank validation or demand adjustment may be the reason.
How-to

How to check on income tax portal

Log into incometax.gov.in, go to e-File, Income Tax Returns, View Filed Returns, then check return and refund status for the assessment year.

How-to

How to check additional refund details

Use refund reissue/status services where available. Keep PAN, assessment year, acknowledgement number and bank details ready.

How-to

Why refund gets stuck

  • Bank account not pre-validated.
  • Name mismatch between PAN and bank account.
  • Account closed or IFSC changed.
  • Outstanding demand adjusted against refund.
  • Refund differs from ITR computation after processing adjustment.
How-to

What to do if refund is stuck

  • Pre-validate bank account.
  • Raise refund reissue request if needed.
  • Check outstanding demand and intimation.
  • Raise grievance if processing is delayed beyond normal period.
