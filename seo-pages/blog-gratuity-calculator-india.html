What this covers

Use this calculator to estimate gratuity and model exemption for covered employees. Actual tax treatment depends on employer type, service continuity and lifetime exemption already used.

  • Formula for covered employees: monthly salary x 15 / 26 x completed years.
  • Government and private-sector tax treatment differ.
  • Private-sector exemption must consider actual amount, formula amount and monetary cap.
