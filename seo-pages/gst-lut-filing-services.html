LUT lets exporters export without upfront IGST payment

Without LUT, exporters may need to pay IGST and claim refund later. LUT in Form RFD-11 is generally valid for one financial year and should be renewed at the beginning of the year.

Service specific

Situations this page is built for

  • New exporter setting up LUT for the first time.
  • Annual LUT renewal due in April.
  • LUT rejected due to demand or compliance issue.
  • Software or IT services company exporting services.
  • Freelancer receiving foreign client payments and needing export GST treatment.
Service specific

Documents and details usually required

  • GSTIN with export turnover history.
  • IEC number for goods exporters.
  • PAN of authorised signatory.
  • No-pending-demand declaration.
  • Digital signature or EVC for GST portal filing.
Service specific

Process

Log in to GST portal

Use authorised signatory credentials.

Open Furnish LUT

Go through Services, User Services or Refunds depending on portal path.

Fill RFD-11

Select financial year and declaration details.

Submit

Use DSC/EVC and download acknowledgement.

Service specific

Common mistakes

  • Not renewing LUT at start of April.
  • Using wrong financial year in form.
  • Not linking IEC with GSTIN for goods exports.
  • Filing with unresolved compliance issues.

Questions People Ask

FAQs

Is LUT required every year?

Yes, LUT is typically furnished for each financial year.

Can I use old LUT for new financial year?

Do not rely on previous-year LUT for new-year exports; renew it for the relevant year.

What happens if I export without LUT?

You may need to pay IGST and claim refund later, depending on facts.

Can WorkIndex help file LUT and set a renewal reminder?

Yes. Mention GSTIN, export type and urgency when posting.
