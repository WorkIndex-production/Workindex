Asset and holding period first

Short-term and long-term rules differ for property, listed equity, unlisted shares, debt funds, gold and crypto. Do not apply one rate to all gains.

Blog

Common saving routes

  • Section 54 for residential property sale reinvestment.
  • Section 54F for other asset sale with house investment.
  • Section 54EC bonds within 6 months, subject to limit.
  • Capital Gains Account Scheme before filing deadline where needed.
  • Tax-loss harvesting and set-off.
  • Use annual equity LTCG threshold where available.
Blog

Common mistakes

  • Missing six-month bond deadline.
  • Buying wrong number/type of houses.
  • Not depositing CGAS before due date.
  • Ignoring surcharge.
  • Treating crypto loss like normal capital loss.
