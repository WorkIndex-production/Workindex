Audit service

Concurrent audit runs alongside transactions, not after

Unlike annual audits, concurrent audit usually happens monthly or weekly. It checks credit, KYC, disbursement, cash, exceptions and operational compliance while records are still fresh.

Questions People Ask

FAQs

Is concurrent audit mandatory for all bank branches?

No. Applicability depends on the institution’s policy and regulatory thresholds, but high-risk/high-volume branches are commonly covered.

How often should concurrent audit happen?

Monthly review is common; high-risk operations may need more frequent checks.

Can a CA firm handle concurrent audit remotely?

Some checks can be remote, but regulated entities may still require branch visits or secure data-room access.

Can WorkIndex help find RBI-experienced concurrent auditors?

Yes. Mention sector, branch count, portfolio size and periodicity when posting.
