Documents checklist

From your employer

  • Form 16 Part A for TDS deducted and deposited.
  • Form 16 Part B for salary breakup.
  • Salary slips for all months if Form 16 is not issued.
  • Relieving letter and previous employer Form 16 if you changed jobs.
Documents checklist

From income tax portal

  • Form 26AS to verify TDS credits.
  • AIS to check salary, interest, dividends, capital gains and other reported income.
  • Previous year ITR acknowledgment for reference.
Documents checklist

For deduction claims

  • 80C proofs: ELSS, PPF, LIC, school fees, home loan principal.
  • 80D health insurance premium receipts.
  • HRA rent receipts, rent agreement and landlord PAN where required.
  • Home loan interest certificate.
  • NPS contribution statement.
  • 80G donation receipts with registration details.
Documents checklist

For other income

  • FD interest certificates.
  • Savings account interest.
  • Capital gains statement from broker or mutual fund platform.
  • Dividend statements and foreign income records if any.

Last fact-checked: 25 May 2026.

AY 2026-27 means FY 2025-26 income under the Income-tax Act, 1961. Tax Year 2026-27 means FY 2026-27 income under the Income Tax Act, 2025. Do not mix the two labels.

For AY 2026-27, check the ITR utility, validation rules and official e-filing guidance before relying on secondary summaries.

Use official portal pages, CBDT notifications, the supplied Act PDF and ICAI material before making a filing, payroll, TDS/TCS or rebate decision.
