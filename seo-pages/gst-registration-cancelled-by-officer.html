Problem help

Officer cancellation is different from voluntary cancellation

When a GST officer cancels registration for non-filing, suspicious address or non-compliance, the taxpayer generally needs to apply for revocation within prescribed timelines and clear pending compliance.

Problem help

Common reasons officer cancels registration

  • Non-filing of returns for several periods.
  • Principal place of business found non-existent.
  • Mismatch in Aadhaar or address documents.
  • Registration obtained fraudulently.
  • Business found non-operational.

What is the difference between cancellation and suspension?

Suspension temporarily blocks normal operations while cancellation ends active GSTIN status unless restored.

What if 90 days have already passed?

Remedies become more limited and may require appeal or legal review.

Do I need to pay all pending returns before revocation?

Usually pending compliance must be cleaned up before restoration.
