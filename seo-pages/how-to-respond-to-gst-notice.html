How-to

Every GST notice type has a different response window

SCN, ASMT-10 scrutiny, audit notice, recovery and demand notices each have different legal basis and response expectations. The section number and deadline decide urgency.

How-to

Step 1 - Read the notice carefully

Check section number, financial year, demand amount, exact issue, DIN, response deadline and whether a personal hearing is listed.

How-to

Step 2 - Gather evidence

  • GSTR-1, GSTR-3B and GSTR-2B for the period.
  • Books ledger and invoice register.
  • Purchase invoices and payment proof for ITC disputes.
  • E-way bills, contracts or work orders.
  • Previous responses and portal acknowledgements.
How-to

Step 3 - File response on GST portal

Log into GST portal, open notices and orders, upload a point-wise reply with supporting documents, then download the acknowledgement.

How-to

Step 4 - Attend personal hearing if requested

For large demands or fraud/suppression allegations, attend with a CA/consultant and keep hearing notes and acknowledgement.

How-to

Common mistakes

  • Responding without matching legal ground.
  • Missing deadline and getting ex-parte order.
  • Paying without verifying demand.
  • Not saving portal acknowledgement.

Records Checklist

Keep these ready before requesting quotes

  • PAN, Aadhaar, GSTIN, TAN, DIN, CIN, LLPIN, UAN or registration number as applicable.
  • Portal login access or screenshots from Income Tax, GST, MCA, EPFO, ESIC, Startup India or other relevant portals.
  • Previous filings, acknowledgements, challans, certificates, orders, notices, audit reports and working papers.
  • Bank statements, invoices, contracts, payroll records, books export, rent agreements, transaction reports or board/investor documents relevant to the case.
  • A short written summary of what happened, what deadline exists, what help you need and whether support can be remote or must be local.

Should I reply myself to a GST notice?

Small clarifications may be manageable, but ITC reversal, demand, audit or fraud allegations need expert help.

What is ASMT-10?

It is a scrutiny notice asking for explanation of return discrepancies.

What documents should I upload?

Upload only relevant reconciliations, invoices, ledgers and explanations tied to each notice point.
