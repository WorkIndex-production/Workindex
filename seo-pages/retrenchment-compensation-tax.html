Info guide

What this covers

Retrenchment compensation is different from resignation, VRS and gratuity. Tax exemption depends on legal character, wages, service period and employer documentation.

  • The legal reason in the termination letter matters.
  • The exemption is generally least of actual compensation, formula amount and cap.
  • VRS and retrenchment use different legal routes.
  • Employer Form 16 split should be reviewed before ITR.
Use cases

Who this is for

  • Employee affected by restructuring.
  • Factory worker retrenched in layoff.
  • Startup employee receiving severance.
  • CA checking taxable portion.
Records

Documents and data to verify

  • Termination/retrenchment letter.
  • Salary and service-period computation.
  • Compensation breakup.
  • Form 16 and bank proof.
Care points

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Calling every severance payment retrenchment.
  • No formula working.
  • Treating excess over cap as exempt.
  • Mixing VRS and retrenchment without documentation.
Action

How to proceed

  • Confirm the applicable financial year, assessment year, taxpayer type, state and portal status before acting.
  • Reconcile portal data with books, AIS/Form 26AS, GST returns, contracts, invoices, bank statements and source documents.
  • Prepare a written computation, checklist, filing note or response with assumptions clearly stated.
  • Download acknowledgements, challans, workings and evidence after filing or submission.
