What this covers

Power BI developers help businesses turn accounting, sales, operations, marketing and finance data into dashboards, automated reports and decision workflows.

  • Power BI work includes data modelling, DAX measures, Power Query, report design and refresh setup.
  • Finance dashboards need clean chart of accounts and KPI definitions.
  • Data sources may include Excel, SQL, ERP, Zoho, Tally exports, Google Sheets and APIs.
  • Access control, refresh schedule and ownership should be agreed before build.

Use cases

Who this is for

  • CFO dashboard for monthly MIS.
  • Sales dashboard for regional teams.
  • Operations dashboard with automated refresh.
  • Startup investor metrics reporting.
Records

Documents and data to verify

  • Sample data and source systems.
  • KPI definitions and desired visuals.
  • User roles and access needs.
  • Refresh frequency and hosting preference.
Care points

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Building visuals before cleaning data model.
  • No DAX ownership or documentation.
  • Unclear refresh responsibility.
  • Sharing sensitive data without access rules.
Action

How to proceed

  • Confirm the applicable financial year, assessment year, taxpayer type, state and portal status before acting.
  • Reconcile portal data with books, AIS/Form 26AS, GST returns, contracts, invoices, bank statements and source documents.
  • Prepare a written computation, checklist, filing note or response with assumptions clearly stated.
  • Download acknowledgements, challans, workings and evidence after filing or submission.
