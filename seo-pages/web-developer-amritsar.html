What this covers

Amritsar web projects often serve hospitality, pilgrimage tourism, textile/hosiery businesses, NRI services and regional ecommerce brands.

  • Hospitality sites need booking/enquiry flows and local SEO.
  • Textile businesses may need catalogues and wholesale enquiry forms.
  • NRI-focused services need trust signals and documentation pages.
  • Project scope should include content, photography, hosting and maintenance.
Use cases

Who this is for

  • Hotel or travel business website.
  • Textile catalogue site.
  • NRI service provider website.
  • Retail ecommerce launch.
Records

Documents and data to verify

  • Service/product catalogue.
  • Photos, pricing and contact details.
  • Domain/hosting access.
  • SEO keywords and city targets.
Care points

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Using generic stock content.
  • No WhatsApp/enquiry tracking.
  • Slow mobile performance.
  • No local business schema or map setup.
Action

How to proceed

  • Confirm the applicable financial year, assessment year, taxpayer type, state and portal status before acting.
  • Reconcile portal data with books, AIS/Form 26AS, GST returns, contracts, invoices, bank statements and source documents.
  • Prepare a written computation, checklist, filing note or response with assumptions clearly stated.
  • Download acknowledgements, challans, workings and evidence after filing or submission.
