Is interest earned on NRE accounts taxable in India?

No. Interest earned on Non-Resident External (NRE) accounts is fully exempt from income tax in India under Section 10(4)(ii).

What forms are needed for outward remittance from an NRO account?

Remitting funds from an NRO account (e.g. sale proceeds of inherited property) requires filing Form 15CA online and obtaining a CA-certified Form 15CB verifying tax compliance.
