Niche segment

Real estate GST is project-type dependent

Under-construction residential, affordable housing, commercial construction, mixed-use projects, JDA and long-term lease can all have different GST treatment and ITC rules.

Niche segment

Situations this page is built for

  • Builder starting new residential project.
  • Developer in JDA with landowner.
  • Project crossing RERA and GST setup at the same time.
  • ITC on cement, steel and services needs reversal review.
  • Completion certificate issued and future GST liability unclear.
Niche segment

Documents and details required

  • Project RERA registration.
  • Sale agreement and booking structure.
  • Completion certificate timeline.
  • Input purchase invoices.
  • JDA agreement where applicable.
  • Previous GST returns for ongoing project.
