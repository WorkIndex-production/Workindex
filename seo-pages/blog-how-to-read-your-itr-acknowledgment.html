How to Read Your ITR Acknowledgment (ITR-V)
What each field means after filing

ITR-V confirms filing details, but you still need to check assessment year, acknowledgement number, income, tax paid, refund and verification status.

When this page is relevant

  • First-time filer checking whether filing succeeded.
  • Taxpayer asked for acknowledgement by bank, employer or CA.
  • Someone received a notice and needs to trace original return.
  • Return filed but not verified within deadline.
Blog

What ITR-V confirms

  • Acknowledgement number.
  • Filing date.
  • Assessment year.
  • ITR form used.
  • Gross total income.
  • Total tax paid.
  • Refund claimed or tax payable.
  • E-verification status.
Blog

Key fields to verify immediately

  • Assessment year, not just financial year.
  • Gross total income matching computation.
  • Tax paid matching challan and TDS.
  • Bank account number for refund.
  • PAN and contact details.
Blog

E-verification versus physical ITR-V

E-verify within the allowed period using Aadhaar OTP, net banking, DSC or other approved methods. If not e-verified, the return can become invalid; physical submission to CPC applies only where permitted.

Blog

What to do if ITR-V shows wrong data

If the return itself is wrong, file a revised return within the permitted window. If only verification is pending, complete verification or reissue steps as applicable.

For AY 2026-27, check the ITR utility, validation rules and official e-filing guidance before relying on secondary summaries.

What to verify for How to Read Your ITR Acknowledgment (ITR-V)

  • Correct financial year, assessment year or tax year.
  • Taxpayer type, age category, residential status and business/profession status.
  • Exact income heads, including salary, house property, business/profession, capital gains, VDA and other sources.
