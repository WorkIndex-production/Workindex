What transfer pricing FAQ covers

Indian transfer pricing applies to specified international transactions and certain domestic transactions. It requires arm’s length pricing, documentation and Form 3CEB where applicable.

FAQs

What is transfer pricing?

It is the process of pricing related-party transactions at arm’s length so profits are not shifted improperly.

Who needs Form 3CEB?

Taxpayers with reportable international or specified domestic transactions generally need accountant certification in Form 3CEB.
