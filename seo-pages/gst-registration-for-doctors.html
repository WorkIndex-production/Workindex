Most clinical services are GST-exempt, but not everything

Healthcare services by authorised medical practitioners and clinical establishments are generally exempt. Separate taxable income streams can arise from pharma speaking fees, consulting, product promotion, pharmacy sales, diagnostic equipment/reagent supplies, or non-clinical platform income.

Common mistakes

  • Registering when all income is genuine exempt clinical services.
  • Not registering when pharma consulting income crosses threshold.
  • Mixing exempt clinical fees and taxable consulting in the same invoice.
  • Claiming ITC against exempt healthcare income.

Questions People Ask

FAQs

Is hospital income taxable under GST?

Core healthcare services are generally exempt, but non-healthcare income streams need separate review.

Does a solo practitioner need GSTIN?

Not if only exempt healthcare services are provided, but taxable consulting or goods supply can change this.

What about telemedicine app income?

It depends on contract, service nature, recipient and whether the income remains exempt healthcare.
