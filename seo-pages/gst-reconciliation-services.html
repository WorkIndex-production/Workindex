Service hub

What this covers

GST reconciliation matches portal data with books, invoices, vendor filings and ITC claims. With IMS/ECRS workflows, monthly discipline matters more than year-end cleanup.

  • GSTR-2B vs purchase register matching.
  • GSTR-1 vs sales register matching.
  • IMS action review before return finalisation.
  • Annual GSTR-9 reconciliation.
Use cases

Who this is for

  • Business with ITC mismatch.
  • Company preparing GSTR-9.
  • New accountant cleaning old GST data.
  • Ecommerce seller with settlement complexity.
Records

Documents and data to verify

  • GST portal reports.
  • Purchase and sales register.
  • GSTR-1, 3B and 2B downloads.
  • Credit/debit notes.
Care points

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Claiming unmatched ITC.
  • No supplier follow-up.
  • Ignoring credit notes.
  • Reconciling only annually.
Action

How to proceed

  • Confirm the applicable year, taxpayer type, state, registration status and portal form before acting.
  • Reconcile the official portal data with books, bank statements, certificates, invoices and notices.
  • Prepare a written computation, filing note, document checklist or response with assumptions clearly stated.
  • Download acknowledgements, challans, workings and evidence after filing or submission.
